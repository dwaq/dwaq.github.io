I'm a tinkerer through and though...

...at work...

I have a passion for electronics inside and outside of work. At Hughes Network Systems, I work in the hardware division creating and testing new designs for broadband satellite modems. I am familiar with the design process from initial requirements, to high- and low-level designs, to schematic capture using Cadence tools, to verification and testing, and finally to product support. I taught myself the scripting language Python to automate testing in order to save time and reduce errors. I have recently been involved in firmware development for the STM32F0 family of ARM microcontrollers for two projects. Both involve heavy use of peripherals and strict timing and are an essential part of each product's success.

...and at play

Outside of work, I love to "tinker" with electronics. I spend my free time designing and building electronic circuits. I also have a Printrbot 3D printer and enjoy creating objects layer-by-layer. My love of microcontrollers (and learning new programming languages) carries outside of work and many of my hobby projects take advantage of those skills.
